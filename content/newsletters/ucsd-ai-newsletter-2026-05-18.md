## What's New in Your AI Tools

### TritonGPT

-   **[TritonGPT Widget gets live campus data](https://tritonai.ucsd.edu/tritongpt/release-notes/5-1-2026-release.html)** — The chat widget now shows real-time parking availability and Geisel Library busyness levels. Redesigned interface makes it easier to follow how the AI assembles answers.

### Zoom AI Companion

-   **[Zoom Workplace 6.31 update](https://support.zoom.com/hc/en/article?id=zm_kb&sysparm_article=KB0083515)** — Meeting summaries now available in 36 languages. AI Companion can also draft calendar invites directly from meeting discussion.

### Microsoft Copilot with Data Protection

-   **[Federated Copilot connectors now available](https://techcommunity.microsoft.com/blog/microsoft365copilotblog/federated-copilot-connectors---bringing-real-time-enterprise-data-within-microso/4515993)** — Connect Copilot to external data sources like HubSpot, Notion, and more. Ask questions using natural language instead of navigating dashboards.

### Copilot for Microsoft 365

-   **[GPT-5.5 Instant now rolling out](https://techcommunity.microsoft.com/blog/microsoft365copilotblog/available-today-gpt-5-5-instant-in-microsoft-365-copilot/4517084)** — Faster response times with improved reasoning. Write drafts in Word, analyze spreadsheets in Excel, and summarize emails in Outlook with less waiting.

-   **[Agent Mode in Word, Excel, and PowerPoint](https://techcommunity.microsoft.com/blog/microsoft365copilotblog/cowork-in-progress/4511672)** — Copilot can now take actions on your behalf like formatting documents and applying Excel formulas. Less typing, more finishing.

### Google Gemini & NotebookLM

-   **[Gemini 3.1 Pro and Flash available](https://blog.google/technology/ai/)** — Gemini 3.1 brings improved code generation and multi-step reasoning. NotebookLM now generates interactive audio overviews with Q&A style narration.
