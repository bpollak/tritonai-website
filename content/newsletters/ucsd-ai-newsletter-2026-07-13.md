## Monday, July 13, 2026

## What's New in Your AI Tools

### Zoom AI Companion

**[AI catch-up prompts help late joiners get up to speed](https://support.zoom.com/hc/en/article?id=zm_kb&sysparm_article=KB0061222)** — Participants joining a Zoom meeting late now see an automatic pop-up offering to use AI to summarize what they missed. When Meeting Questions is enabled, late arrivals get instant context without interrupting the meeting flow.

### Copilot for Microsoft 365

**[OpenAI's GPT-5.6 is now available in Microsoft 365 Copilot](https://techcommunity.microsoft.com/blog/microsoft365copilotblog/available-today-openai%E2%80%99s-gpt-5-6-in-microsoft-365-copilot/4533152)** — Rolling out in Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Copilot Chat, and Copilot Cowork. GPT-5.6 brings stronger reasoning for multi-step tasks — cleaner drafts in Word, sharper analysis in Excel, richer presentations in PowerPoint. In Copilot Cowork, it powers end-to-end agentic workflows that move from instruction to completed deliverable.

### Google Gemini & NotebookLM

**[NotebookLM adds video overviews and structured research outputs](https://blog.google/technology/ai/notebooklm/)** — NotebookLM can now generate AI-powered video summaries alongside Audio Overviews, and supports structured outputs like FAQs, timelines, tables, and flashcards. Combined with larger source limits and Gemini 3.5 capabilities, it's a stronger research and analysis tool for campus workflows.
