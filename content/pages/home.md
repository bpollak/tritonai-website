## One ecosystem, several ways to deliver value

TritonAI combines a broadly useful AI assistant with focused solutions for campus workflows. A common platform provides model choice, retrieval from approved knowledge, developer interfaces, and operational visibility. Teams can start with a conversation, embed an assistant in an existing service, or develop a supervised workflow for a repeatable problem.
