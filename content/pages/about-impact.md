## Publication standard

Every public quantitative claim must include a definition, source, owner, measurement period, last-reviewed date, and enough context to distinguish a controlled pilot from a production service. Reported figures from presentations remain unpublished until the responsible owner validates that package.

## Current public evidence

The use-case library publishes delivery status and each workflow's intended measurement plan. Quantitative results will appear here when their definitions and sources are approved for public use. This avoids presenting a promising pilot result as a campus-wide outcome.
