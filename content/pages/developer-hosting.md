## Hosting choice for AI models

TritonAI Developer APIs can route to approved commercial cloud services or open models hosted on UC-controlled infrastructure. Availability and permitted data depend on the selected service and its approved controls. Review the current model hub and applicable campus requirements before implementation.

## Current pilot boundary

The emerging campus application-hosting lane is intended for approved internal applications. It should not be represented as a general-purpose home for public websites, regulated health information, or payment processing without a separately approved service and control set.
