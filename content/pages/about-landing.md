<p class="lead landing-hub-lead">TritonAI is UC San Diego's shared ecosystem for trusted AI services, practical learning, governed development, and human-supervised solutions to recurring campus problems.</p>

<section class="cms-text-block" aria-labelledby="about-mission-heading">
<div class="row"><div class="col-md-8">
<p class="home-kicker">Mission and vision</p>
<h2 id="about-mission-heading">Make useful AI a trusted campus capability</h2>
<p>UC San Diego is building shared foundations instead of isolated pilots: approved model access, reusable knowledge, developer interfaces, practical training, and clear accountability for the services that surround AI.</p>
<p><a class="btn btn-default" href="/about/strategy.html">Read the TritonAI strategy</a></p>
</div></div>
</section>
<section class="landing-section" aria-labelledby="strategy-priorities-heading">
<div class="landing-section-heading"><p class="home-kicker">How we work</p><h2 id="strategy-priorities-heading">Six priorities shape the program</h2><p>Each priority keeps the technology connected to campus needs, responsible ownership, and evidence.</p></div>
<div class="row cms-tile-grid">
<div class="col-sm-6 col-md-4"><article class="cms-link-tile"><span class="glyphicon glyphicon-lock" aria-hidden="true"></span><h3>Governed infrastructure</h3><p>Match identity, hosting, data handling, and review controls to the service and its risk.</p></article></div>
<div class="col-sm-6 col-md-4"><article class="cms-link-tile"><span class="glyphicon glyphicon-globe" aria-hidden="true"></span><h3>Shared access</h3><p>Offer common platforms and patterns so departments can build on a trusted foundation.</p></article></div>
<div class="col-sm-6 col-md-4"><article class="cms-link-tile"><span class="glyphicon glyphicon-screenshot" aria-hidden="true"></span><h3>Specific pain points</h3><p>Start with a recurring problem, an accountable owner, and an outcome worth measuring.</p></article></div>
<div class="col-sm-6 col-md-4"><article class="cms-link-tile"><span class="glyphicon glyphicon-link" aria-hidden="true"></span><h3>Meet the workflow</h3><p>Bring AI into the services and tools people already use instead of creating extra steps.</p></article></div>
<div class="col-sm-6 col-md-4"><article class="cms-link-tile"><span class="glyphicon glyphicon-random" aria-hidden="true"></span><h3>Model flexibility</h3><p>Use approved hosting choices and a common gateway rather than depending on one model vendor.</p></article></div>
<div class="col-sm-6 col-md-4"><article class="cms-link-tile"><span class="glyphicon glyphicon-refresh" aria-hidden="true"></span><h3>Prepare for agency</h3><p>Develop skills, tools, evaluation, and oversight for supervised multi-step work.</p></article></div>
</div>
</section>
