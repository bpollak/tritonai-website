AI Webinars

This webinar series showcases how AI is being used across campus to streamline workflows, support decision-making, and enhance learning. Each session features concrete examples, demonstrations, and guidance to help you build confidence using AI in your day-to-day work.

AI Webinar #6 - One Year of Innovation with TritonGPT: Past, Present, and Future

Discover how far we've come and where we're headed! In this webinar we'll reflect on TritonGPT's launch and showcase exciting new features like "MyDocuments", model switching, chat searching, and chat link sharing. Additionally, we will share strategies to remain engaged with AI topics.

Watch the Webinar #6 Recording

AI Webinar #5 - Lean Six Sigma vs. Artificial Intelligencence

This webinar highlights the core principles of Lean Six Sigma and demonstrates how AI-driven tools like UC San Diego’s TritonGPT can optimize workflows and problem-solving. Through real-world examples and impact data, the session explores how these tools differ, how they may complement each other, and potential practical applications.
