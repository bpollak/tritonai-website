TritonGPT: The Platform at the Center of TritonAI

  1. TritonAI
  2. TritonGPT

Trusted AI with approved hosting choices

TritonGPT is the platform layer at the center of TritonAI — UC San Diego's approach to making AI useful across the university. It supports open models hosted on UC-controlled infrastructure at the San Diego Supercomputer Center and approved enterprise cloud models. The selected route depends on the capability, data, and controls approved for the service.

TritonGPT is available to eligible UC San Diego students, faculty, and staff at no additional cost. The platform now spans:

  • Specialized Assistants — task-specific tools built around real university workflows (job descriptions, fund management, phishing analysis, scheduling, and more)
  • Model Choice — self-hosted and approved cloud models through a governed LiteLLM gateway, with 20+ models available
  • Live Campus Data — parking availability, library busyness, and enterprise data warehouse queries via the Enterprise Data Agent
  • Developer Access — APIs, starter credits, usage reporting, and recharge paths for campus builders
  • Embedded Support — AI widget on Blink and campus websites, now in the UC San Diego mobile app
  • Instructional AI — course-grounded assistants for teaching and learning
  • Reusable Skills & MCP — a growing toolkit of connectors and workflow patterns, including the UCSD Skills Library

Assistants can retrieve relevant context from approved public and campus knowledge sources at request time, helping users receive more useful answers without describing that content as model training.

The current arc is broader than chat: it's about shared infrastructure, model flexibility without fragmented governance, and the building blocks for supervised agent workflows through the TritonAI Harness.

Access TritonGPT

TritonGPT is available to all UC San Diego faculty, staff, and students at no cost. Sign in with your UC San Diego credentials to access specialized assistants, model switching, document uploads, and campus-aware AI tools.
