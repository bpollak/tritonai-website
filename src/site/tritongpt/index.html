TritonGPT: AI Powered Support

  1. TritonAI
  2. TritonGPT

Secure UC San Diego–hosted AI platform 

TritonGPT operates from the secure confines of the San Diego Supercomputer Center. This secure, on-premises hosting ensures UC San Diego's complete control over data usage and sharing.

TritonGPT's Assistants are tailored for use in administrative roles and adept at handling university-specific questions, summarizing documents, and more, thanks to its training on extensive public-facing university information. This focus enables TritonGPT to provide accurate and relevant information to UC San Diego users.

TritonGPT is available to campus employees at no cost.

Access TritonGPT

TritonGPT access is currently available for faculty, staff, and students within UC San Diego Campus and Health Sciences.
