August 13, 2024 Release Details

Check out this helpful tutorial to explore the latest features now available in TritonGPT.

New AI Assistant: Fund Manager Coach

Fund Manager Coach (beta) is designed to provide helpful and accurate information on a range of finance and research-related topics, including budgeting, grant guidelines, financial transactions, payroll, and compliance for research proposals and grant spending.

Enhanced User Experience

  • New UI Design: Enjoy a refreshed and modern look with the updated theme, featuring a cleaner and lighter design.
  • Customizable Sidebar: Pin and unpin the sidebar to suit your workflow and preferences, giving you more control over your workspace.

Upgrade to the latest Llama 3.1 Large Language Model (LLM)

  • Improved accuracy and reliability: The upgrade to Llama 3.1 brings enhanced language understanding and generation capabilities, resulting in even more accurate and reliable responses to user queries.
  • Enhanced reasoning and problem-solving: Llama 3.1 includes significant improvements to its reasoning and problem-solving abilities, enabling TritonGPT to better understand complex relationships, make more informed decisions, and provide more insightful and logical responses.

Document Upload

Upload and interact with the content of a file using an AI Assistant. Choose the right Assistant for your needs:

  • General AI Assistant: Ideal for general knowledge and information. Use this Assistant if you don't need UC San Diego-specific context.
  • UC San Diego Assistant: Perfect for incorporating UC San Diego-specific knowledge and context into your interactions with TritonGPT.

Supported File Types for File Uploads:

  • .pdf (Portable Document Format)
  • .docx (Microsoft Word Document)
  • .txt (Plain Text File)
  • .xlsx (Microsoft Excel Spreadsheet)
  • .pptx (Microsoft PowerPoint Presentation)
  • .csv (Comma Separated Values)
  • .json (JavaScript Object Notation)
  • .xml (Extensible Markup Language)
  • .md (Markdown File)
  • .yaml (YAML Ain't Markup Language) and .yml (YAML Ain't Markup Language)
  • .eml (Email File)
  • .epub (Electronic Publication)
  • .conf (Configuration File)
  • .mdx (Markdown Extended)

Input Limits for file uploads:

TritonGPT's input window size has limitations, but can still process a significant amount of text. The approximate input limits are:

  • Document Types and Limits
    • Word Document: 106 KB, about 12 pages 
    • PDF document: 2 MB, about 30 pages 
    • Excel Table: 42 KB, about 300 rows, 8 data columns 

Note: These limits are approximate and may vary depending on the complexity of the input text.

Switch Between Assistants

Switch between Assistants within a chat flow:

To trigger a specific Assistant, simply type the "@" symbol followed by the Assistant's name. For example:

  • Start with the General AI Assistant and want to switch to the UC San Diego Assistant? Type "@UC San Diego"
  • Want to switch back to the General AI Assistant? Type "@General AI"

This will seamlessly transition the conversation to the new Assistant, allowing you to access its unique capabilities.

Saved Chats

Save and Organize Your Chats:

  • Save chats to a folder for easy access and organization
  • Normally, chats are retained for 90 days. To keep a chat for longer than 90 days, save it to a folder before the retention period expires.

Search and Chat

  • Search: Quickly find answers to your questions by searching across all documents and websites you have access to.
  • Chat: Generate new text based on the context of the current Assistant, perfect for creating content, drafting emails, or exploring ideas.

Prompt Gallery

Type the "/" symbol to open the Prompt Gallery, where you can:

  • Browse and reuse previously created prompts
  • Easily access your saved prompts for future conversations

Note: Your prompts are private and only accessible to you, ensuring your conversations and ideas remain secure.

See Context

Explore the sources behind an Assistant's response:

  • View source documents and web pages: See the original sources that informed the Assistant's answer, providing transparency and context.
  • Understand the matching scores: Get insight into the algorithm's decision-making process by viewing the scores that determined the relevance of each source document to your prompt.
  • Interact with specific sources: Select the documents that interest you the most and engage with them directly within the chat flow, allowing for a deeper dive into the topic.

Share Your Chat

Easily generate a shareable link that allows you to share your conversation. Recipients will need to have access to TritonGPT, and with the link, they'll be able to:

  • View your original prompt(s)
  • See the responses from the Assistant
  • Engage with the conversation within the chat flow

Note: Recipients will only be able to view the conversation, not edit or modify it.
