November 24, 2025 Release Details

Overview

This release brings significant updates to TritonGPT, featuring a refreshed Chat UI, an enhancement that organizes Documents and Chat Folders into "Projects”, and the introduction of next-generation models like GPT-5.1 and Gemini 3 Pro. Additionally, this version introduces a powerful new Deep Research tool and enhanced personalization features to make the platform work better for you.  See these changes highlighted through our interactive button guide.

What's New and Changed

Projects: Your Personal AI Workspace

  • Unified Organization: "My Documents" and "Chat Folders" have been consolidated into a single feature called Projects. This unifies your files and conversation history into one streamlined workspace.
  • Personalized Agents: Specific custom instructions can now be added directly within a Project. This allows you to tailor the behavior of TritonGPT for that specific project, effectively turning it into your own personal agent or specialized assistant for that topic.

Personalization

  • Control Your Experience: Manage your TritonGPT interactions by clicking on your name in the bottom-left corner of the screen.
  • Default Model Selection: Choose your preferred default LLM.
  • Memory & Customization: This new menu enables specific "Personalizations" and "Memory." These preferences are integrated into the system prompt while you use TritonGPT, tailoring the AI's responses to your specific needs and context.
  • Smart Defaults: To get you started, the system automatically populates your Display Name and UCSD Affiliation. However, you retain full control to remove or edit these details to best suit your preferences.

Deep Research

  • Search Capabilities Return: Internet search functionality is back in TritonGPT, allowing for up-to-date information retrieval directly within the platform. Use this feature combined with Deep Research to develop rich documents and analysis that combines information from UC San Diego and public sources.
  • How to Access: Access these features by selecting the Internet Search Assistant located within the Agent Selection Panel.

New Models & Enhanced Privacy Labels

  • New Cloud Capabilities: GPT-5.1 and Gemini 3 Pro are now available, offering access to the latest generation of cloud-based AI reasoning.
  • Secure Local Options: Our existing on-premises models, including GPT OSS and Llama 4 Scout, remain available for use.
  • Privacy Transparency: To help you make informed decisions, models are now clearly labeled as "Cloud" or "Local" directly in the interface.
    • Local models are hosted entirely on-premises, meaning your data never leaves the UC San Diego network—providing an extra layer of security for sensitive tasks.

System Performance & Updates

  • Refreshed Interface: The main chat interface has received a visual update for a cleaner, more intuitive experience. This includes fixes for various visual bugs and improved branding consistency throughout the platform.
  • Job Description Helper: The JDH tool has been upgraded to run on our improved backend infrastructure for better reliability.
  • Better Complex Task Handling: The system's ability to handle multi-step requests has been improved, allowing for more complex chains of actions to run seamlessly.
  • Faster Search: Search speeds have been optimized to be faster and even more accurate.
