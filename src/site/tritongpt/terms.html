TritonGPT Terms of Use

TritonGPT is an advanced language model designed to assist and generate human-like text. It is being provided to students as a tool for personal use or for academic or professional use if authorized by the instructor or supervisor. Please read the following Terms of Use to understand how you can responsibly use TritonGPT and what you can expect from our service.

Authorized Users

Only employees and students of UC San Diego campus and health sciences are authorized to use TritonGPT. By agreeing to these Terms, you represent that you are an Authorized User.

Appropriate Use of TritonGPT

TritonGPT is designed to assist the UC San Diego community with their questions and information needs. It is not intended for personal or commercial use, or to generate output that students would submit for credit.

You are not allowed to use TritonGPT for any purpose that is unlawful, prohibited, or otherwise restricted by UC policy (including UC San Diego’s Academic Integrity Policy), guidelines (including those provided by your course instructors or supervisors), or these Terms of Use.

You may not enter any material that:

  • Is defamatory, obscene, indecent, abusive, offensive, harassing, violent, hateful, inflammatory, or otherwise objectionable.
  • May lead to self harm or to others.
  • Promotes sexually explicit or pornographic material, violence, or discrimination based on race, sex, religion, nationality, disability, sexual orientation, or age.
  • Infringes any patent, trademark, trade secret, copyright, or other intellectual property or other rights of any other person.
  • Promotes any unethical or illegal activity, or advocates, promotes, or assists any unethical or unlawful act.

Output Accuracy

Generative Pre-Trained Transformers (like TritonGPT) are trained to generate the next best word in the sentence based on patterns in their training data set. They are neither trained to nor capable of discerning fact from fiction, truth from falsehoods. We do not guarantee that the information provided is always accurate, complete, or up-to-date. We rely on data and information from various sources, and there may be instances where the information is incomplete, outdated, or incorrect, or TritonGPT does not retrieve information from the appropriate source. Do not make critical decisions based solely on information provided through TritonGPT.

Ultimately, you are responsible for verifying the accuracy and appropriateness of the output before you use it. If you have any doubts or concerns about the accuracy of the information provided, please let us know by providing feedback through TritonGPT, and we will do our best to verify it. Administrators review the feedback and use it to help shape the direction of the service. Knowingly providing misleading feedback could result in termination of the service.

You may not submit TritonGPT output as your own work for any form of academic credit unless the instructor has authorized such use. In that case, we recommend keeping a record of your chat history should your instructor ask you for it.

No Personal Advice

TritonGPT is not designed to provide legal, medical, financial, or academic advice to individuals. The information provided is for informational purposes only and should not be used as a substitute for professional advice.
