AI Tools

  1. TritonAI
  2. AI Tools

Overview

TritonAI Tools puts powerful AI capabilities directly in your hands—no coding expertise required. From ready-to-use utilities for everyday tasks to low-code platforms for building custom workflows, we're developing a comprehensive toolkit that empowers faculty, staff, and researchers to work smarter.

Whether you need to transcribe a lecture, generate accessible document alternatives, or build an automated workflow for your department, TritonAI Tools provides the resources to make it happen.

AI Services from supported vendors

AI services for campus employees

Zoom AI Companion

Zoom AI Companion features include meeting transcription, summarization, Q&A, and more. Features need to be enabled via the Zoom web portal. Users are notified when a meeting is AI-enhanced.

Enable Zoom AI Companion

Microsoft Copilot with Data Protection

Copilot with Data Protection is a chatbot that provides a simple chat experience. It supports things like image generation, PDF summarization, content generation, and fact retrieval.

Use Copilot

Copilot for Microsoft 365

Copilot for Microsoft 365 is an AI-powered productivity tool that works alongside popular Microsoft 365 Apps such as Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Outlook, Teams, and more. This product is recharge and currently in a soft launch.

Find out more

Google Gemini & NotebookLM

Google Gemini is a multimodal AI model powering Google's AI chatbot and integrated across its products, capable of understanding and generating diverse data. NotebookLM is an AI-powered research and note-taking tool using Gemini to help users interact with their uploaded documents, offering summarization, Q&A, idea generation, and audio overviews.

Find out more

What's Coming

The AI Tools portfolio is under active development. Sign up for the TritonAI newsletter to be notified when new tools become available, or visit our Training & Resources page to explore what's possible with AI today.

Agent Builder

A low-code workflow automation platform powered by a self-hosted n8n deployment, allowing you to create AI-powered workflows and agents using a simple drag-and-drop interface. Connect data sources, define logic, and deploy custom automations—all without writing code. We are currently in early pilots of this offering.

MCP Server Hub

Expected launch: Summer 2026

Access a growing library of Model Context Protocol (MCP) servers that extend what AI can do for you. MCP enables AI assistants to securely connect with external tools, data sources, and services—unlocking capabilities like querying databases, searching documents, and integrating with campus systems. Our hub will provide campus-ready MCP servers you can leverage in your own projects and workflows.

Agent Skills Library

Expected launch: Summer 2026

A collection of modular, reusable AI capability packages designed for common campus tasks. Agent Skills are pre-built instructions, workflows, and configurations that teach AI assistants how to perform specialized tasks consistently and reliably. Built on an open standard, these skills can work across multiple AI platforms and tools—not just a single ecosystem. Plug them into your projects to accelerate development and ensure high-quality, repeatable results.

Pre-Packaged Tools

Launching throughout 2026

A catalog of ready-to-use AI utilities for common campus needs:

  • Video-to-Text Transcription
  • Text-to-Audio Generation
  • AI-Powered Video Captioning
  • Document Summarization
  • PDF Accessibility Checker
  • Alt Image Text Generator
  • Optical Character Recognition (OCR)

Kuali Build Integration

Pre-built AI blocks that integrate directly into your Kuali Build forms and workflows, bringing intelligent automation to your existing business processes.
